## FINAL TIPS

- The interview is typically 40-60 minutes
- Interviewers are calm and professional — stay composed
- Fundamentals matter more than buzzwords
- If you don't know something, say so honestly rather than bluffing
- Practice explaining your project out loud before the interview
- For every technology you mention, be ready for 2-3 follow-up questions
- Keep answers structured: definition → how it works → where you've used it
